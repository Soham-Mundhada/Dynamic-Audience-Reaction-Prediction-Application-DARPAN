Guidelines for entering the topic :

The search should be as descriptive as possible.
The search should be specific and closely related to the topic.
Use important keywords which relate to the topic.
Avoid the use of generic words which may divert the topic to something else.
Avoid the search text to be biased towards a certain section of the topic; give equal text length-weight to all the sections of the topic.
Avoid the use of grammar forming redundant insignificant words or phrases which don’t have relevance to the topic context.
Don’t use irrelevant character symbols unless necessary.
